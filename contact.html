All figures are calculated using the information you gave us and the largest PV system possible for your home. We take into account your roof information and location to work out annual likely generation using the MCS calculation methodology as suming no shading. The amount of energy you self-consume is estimated from the annual energy usage, annual output of the PV system and when you told us that you use your energy. Savings and earnings are calculated using the above information and assumes a deemed export of 50 % , FIT payments (FIT rates are correct for an install between 1 January 2018 and 31 March 2018 ) and a representative current import energy price.
