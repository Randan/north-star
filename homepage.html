What we do

North Star provides services to maximise energy savings, safety, and security using Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning. We also provide highly competitive financing options specifically for Social Housing and Private Residential properties.

Our latest offering is an Energy as a Service (EaaS) solution for sociaL and privately rented properties, and homeowners with the following hardware installed by professional installers:

  • Solar panels and Batteries
  • IoT sensor pack
  • Smart Thermostat

Our technology and logistics are powered by market leading trusted partners, with guarantees of reliability and efficiency.

My power

MyPower, a combination of rooftop solar photovoltaic (PV) panels and battery storage. This combination increases the 20% to 30% electricity savings enjoyed by households with solar-only to over double.

By installing the highest quality equipment at trade prices via our delivery partner, Travis Perkins Plc means we can install MyPower at prices generally less than others install rooftop solar PV on its own. This guarantees the payback period is the shortest in today’s market.

All installations are performed by accredited and certified local installation engineering firms and the equipment installed is only from leading manufacturer’s carefully selected for reliability and efficiency, all with long equipment warranties.

Just go to our solar calculator to find a detailed system cost and savings breakdown.
